Cartagena is one of the most beautiful cities in Colombia. Located on the Caribbean coast, it combines history, culture, and tropical beaches. The city used to be an important colonial port, and today it attracts visitors from all over the world. You must visit Cartagena if you want to experience colorful streets, local gastronomy, and vibrant nightlife.

Where to Go

The Walled City

You must walk around the historic center and look at the colonial balconies. You can sit down in the plazas and take in the Caribbean atmosphere.

Castillo San Felipe

You have to visit this fortress because it is one of the most important historical sites in Colombia. If you go early, you will avoid large crowds.

What to Do

  • You should take a boat tour to the Rosario Islands.
  • If you book in advance, you will get better prices. (First Conditional)
  • If you travel during high season, prices increase. (Zero Conditional)
  • You must try local food like arepas and coconut rice.
  • You could go out at night and listen to salsa music.

Travel Tips

  • You must wear light clothes because the weather is hot.
  • You have to use sunscreen.
  • You don’t have to rent a car; you can walk everywhere.
  • You must not carry too much cash.
